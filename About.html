About Us

Diagnosed with digestion in November of 2009, John Doe was forced to revisit his diet and make permanent changes to what he consumed. The Medical practicioner and Dietrician instructed John not to consume products that have been treated with certain pesticides and hormones.

John did research regarding the products sold at the supermarkets and he became aware that none of the supermarkets provided fresh uncontaminated products to their customers. John quickly realised that there was a gap in the market that needed to be addressed immediately. John grew up on a farm with his uncle and aunt. He reached out to his uncle and asked him for advise on where to start and what was needed to get this idea off the ground. John sold his car dealership and invested all of his money into buying a piece of land with all of the tools he would need to get started. John's uncle assisted Him in getting the farm going, "sowing, nurturing and ultimately harvesting".

Today, D&H Farmers Market is split into two 2000 acre farms serving around 500 retailer stores, including Pick & Pay, Checkers and Woolworths.

Our Aim

Our Aim is to provide our customers with the highest quality in dairy, meat, vegetable and fruit products. If you are an individual who, like John suffers from a sensitive digestive system please try out our range of products and see if it works for you. Please do not think our products are for sick peope, it is for anyone who would like to switch to a more natural and healthy consumer product. Please feel free to contact us if you have any questions or uncertainties.
